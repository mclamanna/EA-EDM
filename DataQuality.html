Enterprise Data Quality

Understand, improve, protect and govern USPS's Data Quality

Why Enterpise Data Quality?

Data Quality Management refers to the accuracy, timeliness, usability, and relevance of enterprise data used in USPS applications.
Enterprise Data Quality (EDQ) could help in many ways such as:


Reliability

Reliable data that satisfies the business functions and technical requirements of the enterprise.

Consistency

By data being consistent, processes and data management will be cost-effective and will support effective decision-making.
