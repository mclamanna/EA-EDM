FAQ

Q: How often is the golden record data updated?

A- Currently, It is updated on a weekly basis (Every Monday morning)


Q: What if the Facility Data Changes?

A- The changes will be reflected in the weekly updates.


Q: What is the source of the golden record origination?

A- Collaboration of multiple system that includes:

  1. AMS
  2. FDB
  3. EFMS
  4. NASSWEB(future)
  5. FEDSTRIP
