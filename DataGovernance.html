Enterprise Data Governance

Enabling the maintenance and governance of Data

Why Enterpise Data Governance?

Enterprise Data Governance (EDG) is the overall management of the availability, usability, integrity and security of data used in the enterprise. It includes establishing processes to ensure effective data management throughout the enterprise, a method of accountability for the adverse effects of poor data quality and ensures that the data which an enterprise has can be used by the entire organization.
Enterprise Data Governance (EDG) could help in many ways such as:


Increased consistency

Consistency between reports leaves to increased confidence and decision making.

Decreasing the risk of regulatory fines

Data governance defines and enforces the policies around data.

Minimizing re-work

By providing authoritative sources of data, developers know the correct sources of data to use.
